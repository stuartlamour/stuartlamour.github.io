What can I help with?

UX research

Find out what your users think. Help your business goals and users needs align, plan functional and content requirements, design and test information architecture and usability. Make data-driven choices.

Design systems

Evolve your unique visual design language. Create a living Atomic Design ecosystem of reusable components, guided by clear standards for your brand. Build in usability, accessability, typography, colour systems, navigation elements, information design, and interaction design. Create an extendable, future-friendly, and time-saving UI.

Code & developement

Impliment your ideas. From full sites with a CMS, to plugins, all using accessable, reusable, sustainable code, and user-centered design components in HTML, CSS, JS, PHP, and SQL.

About

I've been lucky enough to work with clients including The BBC, Gov.uk, MoMa, The British Museum, The National Portrait Gallery, The NHS, Capita, ARUP, and The Labour Party on digital services. I've created SaaS products for Blackboard, Avado, Titus, and many other e-learning partners. I've worked extensivly with universities and profesional developement providers including Sussex University, University of Strathclyde, The Royal College of GPs, The Royal College of Surgeons, Cass business school, and most recently University Colledge London (UCL) on their online learning.

I'm able to hit the ground running with most projects, and use Jira and Github on a daily basis. I'm equally able to jump into LAMP/MAMP, Python, or JS based project architecture and make an immediate impact on the front end design, usability and accessibility.

I'm happiest mentoring and fostering a culture of Agile UX practice, helping solve problems through lasting culture change, and creating products people really enjoy using.
